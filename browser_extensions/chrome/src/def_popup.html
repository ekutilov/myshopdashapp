myShopDash

Your shopping receipts aggregator and buyers dashboard for Woolworths and Coles

This extension helps you to download all your transactions from your Everyday Rewards (aka Woolworths Rewards) and Coles Web Account: what you bought, when you shopped, and how much you spent on what both in-store and online.

If you scan your loyalty card, Everyday Rewards and Coles save all your shopping receipts in electronic form in your account, and this extension helps you to retrieve and analyse them.

To start using it:

  • For Woolworths, Metro and BWS: navigate to Everyday Rewards My Activity Page,
  • For Coles: navigate to Coles,

log in, and click extension's magic button in the left bottom corner, which will be added there for you. The one like this one:

Your personal shopping data will be securely saved on your computer first.

After the data download finishes, switch to the dashboard web app by pressing “Make it a dashboard” button. The one like this:

Your data will be securely transferred to the myshopdash.app website to be processed and visualised. Your data will be visible to you only and you will be able to wipe it all from there whenever you want.
